Services

Neurology

Our neurology department specializes in diagnosing and treating disorders of the nervous system, including the brain, spinal cord, and nerves. Our team of neurologists provides comprehensive care for conditions such as epilepsy, stroke, multiple sclerosis, and Parkinson's disease.

Eyes care

Our eye care services focus on preserving and enhancing vision health. From routine eye exams to advanced surgical procedures, our ophthalmologists and optometrists offer personalized care for conditions like cataracts, glaucoma, macular degeneration, and refractive errors.

Pulomonary

Our pulmonary specialists are dedicated to diagnosing and treating diseases of the respiratory system. Whether it's asthma, COPD, pneumonia, or sleep apnea, our team provides expert care to improve lung function and overall respiratory health.

Cardiology

Our cardiology department specializes in the prevention, diagnosis, and treatment of heart conditions. Our cardiologists offer comprehensive cardiac care, including screenings, diagnostics, interventions, and management of conditions such as coronary artery disease, arrhythmias, and heart failure.

Dental Care

Our dental care services focus on promoting oral health and wellness. From routine cleanings and check-ups to restorative and cosmetic procedures, our dentists provide personalized care for conditions like cavities, gum disease, dental trauma, and tooth loss.

Hepatology

Our hepatology department specializes in the diagnosis and management of liver diseases. Our hepatologists offer comprehensive care for conditions such as hepatitis, fatty liver disease, cirrhosis, and liver cancer, using advanced diagnostics and innovative treatment approaches.
